Projections and analysis from simulated games

At CustomDFS.com we take a different approach to building projections:

  • Step 1: Set Up Basic Simulation Model

    We take traditional research and analytics (individual stat projections, match-up information, projected player usage, etc) but instead of calculating projections directly we feed them into a sophisticated game simulator.

  • Step 2: Customize Key Stat Categories to Build Unique Simulation Model

    By giving you the freedom to modify projections in key stat categories, you can give each game simulation your own unique "flavor".

  • Step 3: Simulate Thousands of Games

    To account for as many game outcomes as possible we simulate each game THOUSANDS of times based on the custom simulation model that was built based on your inputs. For each simulation we record game results, team stats, and player stats.

  • Step 4: Analyze Data to Develop Projections and Player Interaction Data

    We apply advanced analytical techniques to convert the raw simulation data into useful projections and analysis:

  • Real Ceiling/Floor Analysis

  • Real Player Interactions

Since we base our projections on simulated games, we can provide REAL estimates of each player's ceiling and floor by finding their best and worst performances from among thousands of game simulations.

Since CustomDFS projections are based on realistic game simulations, ALL players in a given game are affected even if you only modify stat projections for a single player!

  • Sounds interesting, but how does it work?

    Step 1: You modify key stat categories based on your knowledge and insight about individual players.

    Step 2: We take your predictions along with baseline stat projections and Vegas information (odds/lines/over-under) to build your own unique simulation model for each game in the slate.

    Step 3: We run thousands of simulations for each game in the slate and record player and team statistics for every game simulation.

    Step 4: We convert the simulation results to a customized set of player projections along with realistic ceiling/floor information.

  • Why are CustomDFS projections based on a simulation - can't you just build them from past performance?

    There are two BIG problems with building a projection model based only on historical data:

    1. Most modeling techniques for player projections are based on some kind of calculation of average historical performance with some measure of variance or standard deviation to calculate floor and ceiling estimates. Unfortunately this really doesn't accurately model what possible results a player could have in a game to give a true projection, floor, and ceiling estimate. By using a simulation we are able to understand the true distribution of all possible player outcomes to give accurate projection, ceiling, and floor estimates.

    2. Most modeling techniques build projections for each player independently and do not account for correlation between players. By running a simulation the interactions between players are REAL so we can do correlation anlaysis, stack analysis, and lineup upside analysis.
